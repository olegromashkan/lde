Our Story

RapidMobil was founded in 2018 with a vision to revolutionize courier services in Moldova. Starting as a small operation in Chisinau, we quickly grew to serve major cities like Balti, Cahul, Tiraspol, and Comrat, driven by our commitment to speed, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

Today, RapidMobil is a trusted name in logistics, offering a range of services from express deliveries within hours to intercity shipments across Moldova. Our team of dedicated couriers and advanced tracking systems ensure your packages arrive safely and on time.

Our Mission

At RapidMobil, our mission is to provide fast, reliable, and affordable courier services that connect people and businesses across Moldova. We strive to exceed customer expectations by combining cutting-edge technology with a customer-first approach, ensuring every delivery is handled with care.
