What are your delivery times?

Standard Delivery in Chisinau takes up to 24 hours. Express Delivery in Chisinau is completed within 3-4 hours. Deliveries across Moldova take 1-2 days, depending on the destination.

Our prices include a base fee plus a per-kilogram rate. For example, Standard Delivery in Chisinau starts at 50 MDL + 2 MDL/kg, while Express is 100 MDL + 5 MDL/kg. Check our Tariffs page for details.
